## Coverage Rules

- The watchlist represents the investment universe under continuous coverage, not only near-term buy candidates.
- Current holdings remain in the watchlist while they are held.
- Exiting a position updates `Dashboard.md` and `account/Current.md`; it does not remove the name from the watchlist by default.
- A name should leave the watchlist only after an explicit decision to stop coverage.
- Every portfolio holding should already exist in the watchlist before it becomes a live position.
- Thesis documents and research notes should deepen coverage, not redefine watchlist membership from scratch.

## Usage Rules

- A watchlist name should exist for a reason, not for general curiosity.
- The watchlist must not be regenerated from the current portfolio.
- If a name was previously researched and still matters, keep it under coverage even when not held.
- A name without a buy or re-entry trigger is not decision-ready.
- A name without a remove-coverage trigger is not governance-ready.
