## Executive Summary

Meituan is the core recovery position of the current portfolio.

The investment thesis is based on improving profitability, resilient free cash flow, strong local services leadership, and long-term AI-driven operating leverage rather than short-term consumption recovery.

The position is intended to become one of the portfolio's long-term compounders.

## Why We Own It

We own Meituan because we believe:

- It remains the dominant platform in China's local services ecosystem.
- Cash flow generation is significantly stronger than market perception.
- Food delivery provides a durable cash engine.
- New initiatives have become more disciplined after cost optimization.
- AI has the potential to improve operational efficiency across logistics, merchant services, and customer support.

## Investment Drivers

### 1. Local Services Leadership

The network effect remains difficult to replicate.

Merchant density, rider network, and user habits together create a durable competitive advantage.

### 2. Cash Flow

The company has entered a phase where free cash flow matters more than GMV growth.

Long-term shareholder returns should increasingly come from capital allocation rather than aggressive expansion.

### 3. AI Opportunity

AI is viewed as an operational multiplier rather than a standalone business.

Potential improvements include:

- Merchant tools
- Rider dispatch
- Customer service
- Advertising efficiency

## Risks

Major risks include:

- Aggressive competition
- Regulatory changes
- Consumption weakness
- Overseas expansion execution
- Margin pressure from new businesses

## Add Conditions

Increase position when:

- Thesis remains unchanged.
- Valuation becomes materially more attractive.
- Market overreacts to short-term news.

## Reduce Conditions

Reduce position only if:

- Competitive advantage weakens.
- Capital allocation deteriorates.
- Long-term cash flow thesis breaks.

Short-term price movements alone are not sufficient reasons to sell.
